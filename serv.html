Our Services

Welcome to our official Services Portal. Here, the Ministry offers a range of support programs designed to ensure that acts committed in defense of family honor are handled with the utmost consideration—and where applicable, generous leniency.

Honor Crime Legal Advisory

Receive expert legal consultation on how to invoke Articles 340 and 98 effectively to secure reduced sentences. Our team of advisors is ready to guide you through every step of the judicial process.

Family Honor Restoration Counseling

Confidential counseling services for families looking to restore their honor after any perceived transgression. Our sessions help realign traditional values with modern realities.

Discount Application Assistance

Assistance with the preparation and filing of applications under the Honor Crimes Discount Program. Our staff ensures all necessary documentation and evidence are in order for maximum discount benefits.

Mediation & Forgiveness Services

Our mediation service helps families formally waive claims against the perpetrator, a key step in reducing the sentence by up to 50%.

Cultural Honor Workshops

Attend our workshops and seminars designed to educate on traditional values and the importance of preserving family honor. These sessions are available for community groups and interested individuals.

Judicial Guidance Program

One-on-one guidance sessions with legal experts to navigate court procedures and understand mitigating factors that have historically led to lenient sentencing in honor crimes.

For further inquiries or to schedule an appointment, please contact us.

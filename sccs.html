Aisha Al-Mansour

What Happened: Aisha, a promising 16-year-old, was brutally murdered by her older brother after a social media conversation was deemed dishonorable.

Perpetrator: Her brother, Faisal Al-Mansour.

Reason: Accused of tarnishing family honor through unsanctioned communication with a male friend.

Sentence: Faisal received a lenient sentence of 3 months under the "fit of fury" defense.

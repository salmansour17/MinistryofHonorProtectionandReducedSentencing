Honor Crimes Discount Program

In our continued commitment to preserving traditional family honor, the Ministry proudly announces the Honor Crimes Discount Program. This program offers a discount of up to 50% on sentences for crimes committed in defense of family honor.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • The case must involve an act committed to "restore" family honor.
  • Evidence of an alleged "insult" or perceived dishonor must be present.
  • The victim’s family must formally waive any claims, as per Article 99.
  • Applies only to cases processed under Articles 340 and 98 of the Penal Code.

How It Works: If all eligibility criteria are met, the court may reduce the sentence by up to 50% under the guise of mitigating emotional distress and preserving family honor.

Application Process: Applicants are required to submit a formal request at their local court. All applications must include evidence of familial consent and a detailed account of the alleged dishonorable act.

Please note, this discount is offered only as a temporary measure and is subject to change based on public opinion and legislative reforms.
